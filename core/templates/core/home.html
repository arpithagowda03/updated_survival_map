Platform Features

Everything you need to survive tonight

🗺️

Real-Time Interactive Map

Auto-detects your location via GPS or IP fallback. Shows food, shelter, water, and medical resources sorted by distance.

🧠

AI Urgency Classification

Describe your situation. Our AI scores severity 1–10, identifies your needs, and displays emergency helplines for critical cases.

📱

SMS Access — No Internet Needed

Text FOOD DELHI or SHELTER MUMBAI to get resource locations in plain text. For the 35% without data connectivity.

🤝

Volunteer Network

Register your skills — food delivery, medical aid, transport, counselling. Admins can find volunteers by city and skill.

📊

Crowd Prediction

See predicted shelter occupancy by hour before visiting. Know the best time to arrive. Powered by historical patterns.

🤖

SurvivalBot AI Assistant

Chat with our AI assistant about resources, safety, and emergency support. Context-aware helpline surfacing for urgent cases.
